Why ATS-Friendly Matters

Over 90% of large companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes. A beautiful resume means nothing if it can't pass through ATS software. Our templates are specifically designed to:

  • Pass ATS parsing with high accuracy
  • Use clean, semantic HTML structure
  • Feature standard section headings and formatting
  • Maintain professional appearance for human reviewers
  • Print perfectly to PDF format

How to Use the Template Builder

  1. Choose a Template: Click "EDIT TEMPLATE" on your preferred style above
  2. Fill the Form: Enter your information in the interactive form
  3. Watch Live Preview: See your resume update in real-time as you type
  4. Download: Click "Download Resume" to get your customized HTML file
  5. Save as PDF: Open the downloaded file and use "Print to PDF" (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)
  6. Submit: Upload your PDF to job applications or email directly
